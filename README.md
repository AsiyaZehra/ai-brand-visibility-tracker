# AI Brand Visibility Tracker 🤖

## Overview
A first-of-its-kind tracking framework that monitors how brands 
and products appear in AI-generated responses across large language 
model (LLM) platforms — including ChatGPT, Perplexity, and 
Google AI Overviews.

As AI search replaces traditional search for millions of users, 
brand visibility in LLM responses is becoming the new SEO frontier. 
This project builds the foundation for measuring and improving that visibility.

## Why This Matters
- 40%+ of users now use AI tools for product discovery
- Traditional SEO metrics don't capture AI-driven brand mentions
- No standardised framework exists yet for AI visibility tracking
- Brands that appear in LLM responses get significant referral traffic

## Tools & Technologies

## Framework Components

### 1. 🔍 AI Mention Monitoring
- Tracks how often brand/product appears in AI responses
- Monitors across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews
- Compares brand vs competitor mentions in LLM outputs
- Logs mention context (positive, neutral, missing)

### 2. 📊 Referral Attribution Analysis
- Identifies traffic coming from AI platforms via GA4
- Separates AI referral traffic from organic search traffic
- Tracks conversion rate of AI-referred visitors
- Monitors trend of AI referrals month over month

### 3. ✍️ Content Optimisation for LLMs
- Analyses what content types get cited by LLMs
- Identifies content gaps causing brand to be missed
- Recommends content structure improvements
- Tracks impact of content changes on AI visibility

### 4. 📈 Visibility Scoring System
- Custom scoring model for AI brand visibility
- Weighted score across platforms (ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overviews)
- Competitor benchmarking dashboard
- Weekly visibility trend reporting
